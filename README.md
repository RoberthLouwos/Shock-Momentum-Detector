# Shock-Momentum-Detector
Pine Script strategy that detects rapid price shocks over a lookback window and trades mean reversion after extreme momentum moves.

---

## Overview
Shock Momentum Detector is a TradingView strategy that identifies rapid price dislocations and attempts to capture short-term reversal opportunities following extreme momentum events.

Designed for intraday and swing research across liquid markets.

---

## Key Features
• Multi-bar shock detection  
• Adjustable sensitivity thresholds  
• Works across timeframes  
• Visual signal markers  
• Lightweight and easy to modify  

---

## Best Use Cases
• Volatility spike trading  
• Mean reversion systems  
• Signal stacking frameworks  
• Research prototyping  

---

## Typical Markets
• US Equities  
• Crypto  
• Futures  
• Forex  

---

## Quick Start
Paste into TradingView Pine Editor → Add to Chart → Adjust Threshold → Test.

## Risk Notice
Markets can trend after shocks. Risk management required for production use.
